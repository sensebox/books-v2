## Technical Detail

* Dimensions: 25mm x 25mm x 9mm
* Weight: 2.4 g
* "Plug-in-and-Go" senseBox compatible
* Operating pressure 300 to 1100 hPa
* Relative precision ± 0.12 hPa
* Absolute precision ± 1 hPa
* Operating power 2.7μA at 1Hz sampling frequency
